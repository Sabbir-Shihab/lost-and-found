About Uttara University Lost & Found

 

Uttara University Lost & Found is a community-based platform designed to help students, faculty members, and staff report, search, and recover lost belongings within the university campus. The system aims to create a safe, supportive, and responsible environment where members of the university community can assist one another through honesty and cooperation.

The platform allows users to post information about lost or found items with descriptions, images, locations, and contact details, making the recovery process faster and more organized. Whether it is an ID card, wallet, mobile phone, documents, keys, or any personal belonging, the system works as a central hub for reconnecting owners with their items.

Our mission is to promote integrity, trust, and social responsibility among students while reducing the stress and inconvenience caused by losing valuable belongings. Through active participation and community support, Uttara University Lost & Found helps build a more connected and caring campus culture.
