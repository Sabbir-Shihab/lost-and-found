Uttara University Lost & Found – User Guidelines

 

 

Welcome to Uttara University Lost & Found

This platform is created to help students, faculty members, and staff recover lost belongings quickly and securely within the university community.

Good Practices for Users

🔹 Provide Accurate Information
When posting a lost or found item, include clear details such as item type, color, location, date, and any unique identification marks.

🔹 Use Clear Photos
Uploading a clear image increases the chance of identifying and returning the item to its rightful owner.

🔹 Respect Privacy & Security
Avoid sharing sensitive personal information publicly. Verify ownership before handing over valuable items.

🔹 Be Honest & Responsible
If you find something, report it honestly. A small act of integrity can make a big difference for someone else.

🔹 Respond Promptly
Check notifications or messages regularly to communicate with the rightful owner or finder.

🔹 Keep the Community Helpful
Use polite and respectful language while communicating with others on the platform.

Positive Impact of the System

✅ Helps students recover important belongings easily
✅ Builds trust and responsibility within the university community
✅ Reduces stress caused by losing valuable items
✅ Encourages honesty and cooperation among students
✅ Creates a safer and more connected campus environment

Remember

“Every lost item has a story — your honesty can help complete it.”
